Troubleshooting

❤️ Health data not showing
  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Health and ensure Vela has permission.
  2. Open the Health app and check that your device is writing data correctly.
  3. Try closing and reopening Vela — data syncs on launch.
  4. Make sure your iPhone is running iOS 16 or later.
⌚ Wearable data not syncing
  1. Open your wearable's companion app and verify it's syncing to Apple Health.
  2. In the Health app, check Data Sources & Access to confirm data is being written.
  3. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled and your device is connected.
  4. Restart both your iPhone and wearable device.
🤖 AI assistant not responding
  1. Check that you have an active internet connection.
  2. Ensure your API key or subscription is valid in Settings.
  3. Try closing and reopening the app.
  4. If the issue persists, the AI service may be experiencing high demand — try again in a few minutes.
📊 Inaccurate health insights
AI insights improve with more data. Wear your device consistently and ensure all relevant HealthKit categories are shared with Vela. If insights seem off, try providing more context when chatting with Avela.
